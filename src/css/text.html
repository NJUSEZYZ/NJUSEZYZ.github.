衬线字体(Serif)、字体大小

With the advent of the computer age, the font has become a part of people's work and life, people will contact it and use it every day. Generally speaking, the birth of a font requires the creative design of the font designer and the stroke-by-stroke production and modification of the font maker. The technical developers encode characters, add program instructions, install libraries, and develop installation programs. The tester performs proofreading, software testing, and compatibility testing on the font, and the production department finalizes the font and finalizes the packaging and marketing. For font manufacturers, what kind of fonts to launch must also go through market research, expert discussions and other links to ensure that the launched font library has market competitiveness. At the same time, the font style and encoding of fonts must also follow the national language. Relevant regulations ensure that font products meet the standards. It takes 2-3 years of hard work to develop a high-quality font library. It is a passionate and creative work that requires all kinds of human, material and financial resources.
